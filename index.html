Red Carpet Hotel

Hotel Red carpet is located in the foot hills of the dhauladhar range in the nature's lap of Kangra Valley. The hotel is situated near by Gyuto Monastery ( karmapa Monastry) surrounded by greenry and small streams. It is well connected with the road network of state highway and very easily accessible . Red carpet Hotel has luxury rooms with individual balconies, all modern facilities, banquet hall, restraunt, lift, excellent food and different cuisines like Indian, Chinese, Tandoor, Continental, South Indian, Thai food. Hotel is situated at 12 km from Gaggal Airport, 5 km from Dharamshala ISBT Bust stand, 12 km from Macleodganj, 6km from Chamunda devi, 83 km from Pathankot Railway Station, 15 km from Kangra, and just walkable distance to Norbulingka Tibetam Institute, Gyuto Monastery (Karmapa Monastry), Vidhan Sabha and Chinmaya Tapovan Ashram.
